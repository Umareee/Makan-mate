Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Catering Inquiries

What is a Mini Buffet?
A Mini Buffet is recommended for 10-15pax. Food will be served in disposable containers, and disposable ware will be provided. Self-heating packs will be provided.

What is a Regular Buffet?
A Regular buffet is recommended for 20pax and above. Full table set-up with warmers and tables with skirting, and disposable ware will be provided.

Are cutleries provided?
Yes, disposable cutleries are provided for all menus. Cutleries are provided 1:1 with additional buffers. Additional disposable cutlery is available at $1.00/set.

Are takeaway boxes/containers provided?
No, we do not provide takeaway boxes/containers for leftover food. For quality and safe consumption, food should not be kept at room temperature for more than 4 hours from the time it is cooked at the caterer’s kitchen to the time it is consumed as advised by NEA.

Are table decorations provided?
All complete buffet set up includes a silk flower centerpiece (unless otherwise indicated). Creative thematic set up services are available at an additional price.

What is the colour of the table skirting for a regular buffet?
The colour of the table skirting is maroon-red.

Are service staff provided?
Standard buffet catering set up does not include service staff. Hiring of service staff is available at S$160/staff for 4 hours with order of regular buffet. We recommend the ratio of 1 service staff to 30 pax.

What is the portion of the food provided like?
We pride ourselves for using only quality ingredients and our servings cater to the exact no. of guests based on your order. As good food runs out fast, we encourage you to order an extra 10% to act as a buffer for your guests.

Are vegetarian meals available?
Yes, we offer vegetarian friendly mini-buffet, buffet and bento sets.

How do I know if my online order is processed?
An autoconfirmation email will be sent after submission and payment of your online order and our sales consultant will contact you if we need any further information from you.

How many days in advance do I need to place an order?
Orders have to be placed 3 working days in advance, before 5pm.

Can I cancel my order after payment has been made?
Yes (minimum 7 working days in advance). However, an administrative charge of $100 will be applicable for cancellation of orders after payment has been made.

Can I make any last minute changes to my order?
Any changes must be made 2 working days prior to the event date.

Will there be any additional charges if I need to make a last minute cancellation of my order?
Only 50% of the total bill will be refunded if order cancellation is made 3 working days prior to the event date. No refund will be made if order cancellation is made less than 48 hours from the event date.

Will you be able to help with last minute orders?
We have a Buffet Express for last minute buffet orders*.
